Introduction to Arduino

An Arduino board

Arduino is an open-source electronics platform based on easy-to-use hardware and software. It was created in 2005 by Massimo Banzi and David Cuartielles to provide a cost-effective way for students and hobbyists to create interactive projects. Since its inception, Arduino has revolutionized the world of electronics and DIY projects by making it accessible to a wider audience

Arduino Radar System

What is an Arduino Radar System?

An Arduino radar system is a radar technology implemented using Arduino microcontrollers. It utilizes radio waves to detect objects, measure distances, and determine their speed and direction. Arduino radar systems are versatile, affordable, and widely used in applications such as obstacle detection, distance measurement, motion sensing, and speed detection. They are popular among hobbyists and engineers for their educational value and practical applications in robotics, automation, and security systems.
